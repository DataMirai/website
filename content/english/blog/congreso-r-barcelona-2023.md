This year we have returned to the II Congress and XIII Conference of the R Hispano Community, which were held in Barcelona and hosted by Pompeu Fabra University.
Meeting up again with familiar faces, catching up and meeting new people with shared interests makes this one of the best (if not the best) events of the year for us.
Holding it at home saved us the hassle of travelling, so we decided to use this time to actively participate with a workshop on using the Tidymodels R library.
We consider it a success, judging by the feedback and questions from the attendees at the end of the presentation, which gave us a real sense of reward for the time and effort invested and makes you want to keep going.

It has certainly been a year to remember, and we're already counting down the days to next year's event!

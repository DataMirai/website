The project [Mataró out loud](https://miraidata.es/en/blog/soroll_mataro/), the scrollstorytelling about noise levels in the city of Mataró, published by Mirai Data, has been recognised with the Català DataViz award and has also been a finalist in the Young Talent category of the Women DataViz 2025 Awards, organised by the [ViT Foundation](https://www.fundaciovit.org/).

The awards ceremony was held at the Casa de la Cultura in Girona, during an evening dedicated to data visualisation and the sector's emerging talent. The awards had three categories and brought together high-quality projects, with very creative and inspiring proposals.

Beyond the recognition received, the experience was particularly enriching for getting to meet other women who also work in the field of data visualisation and sharing ideas, experiences and ways of understanding the visual communication of information.

The submitted projects were of a very high standard, and it was a privilege to be part of an event that highlights the role of women in the world of communication and data visualisation.

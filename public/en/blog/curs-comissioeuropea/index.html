MiraiData provides data training at the European Commission's JRC

  • Mireia Camacho
  • Courses , R
  • April 30, 2025

Last week, MiraiData travelled to Seville to deliver a two-day training course on data analysis, visualisation and communication at the European Commission’s JRC.

On the first day we focused on database management and the fundamentals of visualisation according to each project’s objectives. The second day was more technical: we explored ways to connect to and manage SQL databases and datalakes from R, and we build an interactive application where to explore the data.

We would like to thank the team for their warm welcome and the trust they have placed in us. See you soon!
